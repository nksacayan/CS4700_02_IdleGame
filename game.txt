Let's make a game!
	name:Adventurer's Guild Clicker
	by:Nicholas Sacayan
	desc:An idle game for Professor Summerville's CS 4700.02

Layout
	use default

Buttons
	*TEMPLATE
	  *train
	    name:Train
	    on click:yield 1 attack
			on click:yield 1 health

		*study
	    name:Study
	    on click:yield 1 magic
			on click:yield 1 mana
	*TEMPLATE

Resources
  *health
    name:Health
		show max

	*mana
		name:Mana
		show max

	*attack
		name:Attack

	*magic
		name:Magic

	*xp
		name:XP

	*gold
		name:Gold

	*renown
		name:Renown
		hidden when 0
